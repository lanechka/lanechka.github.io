About

I’m a Canadian who now lives in Norway by way of the United States, Switzerland, and the Netherlands. When not knitting lace, I bake bread, translate German correspondence from World War I, and plot world domination.

Some of my previous jobs include policy analyst for the Ontario provincial government, embassy secretary, and assistant designer at a major Scandinavian yarn company.
