Reviews of Graphic Novels

I review graphic novels (comics) set in World War I as well as Norwegian graphic novels. Most of these books were originally published in French or Norwegian but all my reviews are written in English.


World War I

C'était la guerre des tranchées

This graphic novel by Jacques Tardi is a classic of the genre. Published in 1993, it draws on the experiences of the author's grandfather, who served with the French army during the war.




Norwegian Comics

Magnus Carlsen og Sjakkmorderen

In this infinitely silly and action-filled story, a serial killer has divided Oslo into a chess board and presents world chess champion Magnus Carlsen with chess riddles that he must solve in order to determine the location of the next murder.
