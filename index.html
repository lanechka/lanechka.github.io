News for February 2023

My pattern Olsok has just been released. Leaves and lace celebrate high summer, when Olsok is traditionally celebrated. In Norway, the festival of Olsok commemorates the death of St Olav, who was killed at the Battle of Stiklestad on 29 July 1030. Historically it was an important day for bringing in the hay. The weather on Olsok was also highly anticipated, as it was often used to predict conditions for the autumn and winter.
