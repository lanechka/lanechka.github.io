News for March 2022

The pattern for the Audur shawl is now out on Ravelry. Named for the wife of Gísli Súrsson from the Saga of Gisli the Outlaw, it features a stockinette stitch body border and an Estonian lace border.

The Pattern of the Month for March is Bergtatt. It was inspired by the Norwegian folksong “Margit Hjuxe” in which a troll king kidnaps a young girl in order to marry her.

Both Audur and Bergtatt are 20% off until 31 March 2022; the discount is automatically applied at checkout and you do not need a coupon code.
