News for November 2025

This week's pattern is a reworking of one of my earlier designs, the Maridal shawl, into a scarf. I designed the original triangular shawl for Blacker Yarns a few years ago, and I still love the lacy leaf and cable motif. For the scarf, I used DK weight yarn so it works up quickly to make a chic and snuggly accessory for the winter. And if you're not into scarves or shawls, then matching fingerless mitts are available too.

If you don't use Ravelry, there's now a new option to access my patterns. I've opened a "coffee shop" on ko-fi.com, linked below. So far, you can find all the protest patterns there as well as other selected patterns.
