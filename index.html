News for August 2022

The Pattern of the Month for August is Brisingamen. This cowl features cables and lace and is named for the necklace of the Norse goddess Freya. The motif is easy to memorize, so even though every row is patterned it is a straightforward project. It’s also easy to resize by casting on more/fewer stitches, and lends itself well to different weights of yarn.
