News for September 2025

The pattern for the Treschow fingerless mitts is now available! This sweet design features a Japanese motif with lace and cables, and is a quick knit in fingering weight yarn. The sample was knitted in a woollen-spun yarn, giving the mitts a soft and gentle look, but a denser worsted-spun yarn will really make the cables pop.

I also want to highlight one of my old patterns: the Gunnhild scarf. It features a simple lace pattern that is delineated by twisted-stitch cables. It's best suited to a sleekly-spun sport weight or worsted weight yarn that will bring out the texture of the motifs.

Finally, the last installment (for the foreseeable future) in my All Outta Yarn protest knits series is here! These "Socks for Fighting Fascists" are based on patterns that were originally designed for soldiers who fought Nazis in World War II.
