News for August 2025

The final installment (for the foreseeable future) in my All Outta Yarn protest knits series is here! These "Socks for Fighting Fascists" are based on patterns that were originally designed for soldiers who fought Nazis in World War II.

No Kings was inspired by Suffer-Not-Injustice "Stoneface" Vimes and the rallying cry of the Wee Free Men in Terry Pratchett's Discworld series, as well as recent real-world events.

Poop on Fascists was published earlier this month too. It was designed as a joke, and even if you wouldn't dare to wear it in public, I hope it gives you a laugh.
