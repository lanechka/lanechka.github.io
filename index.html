News for August 2025

No Kings was inspired by Suffer-Not-Injustice "Stoneface" Vimes and the rallying cry of the Wee Free Men in Terry Pratchett's Discworld series, as well as recent real-world events.

Liberal Snowflakes is another recent addition to my series of protest knits. I apologize for the aesthetically jarring placement of the Nelson Mandela quotation on the page with charts. I anticipated haters gleefully telling me about their plans to knit this pattern in complete opposition to the spirit in which it was designed -- as happened with the Eat the Rich hat -- so I decided to try to make the experience as unpleasant for them as I could.

Poop on Fascists was published earlier this month too. It was designed as a joke, and even if you wouldn't dare to wear it in public, I hope it gives you a laugh.
