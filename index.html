News for August 2025

Liberal Snowflakes is the latest addition to my series of protest knits. I apologize for the aesthetically jarring placement of the Nelson Mandela quotation on the page with charts. I anticipated haters gleefully telling me about their plans to knit this pattern in complete opposition to the spirit in which it was designed -- as happened with the Eat the Rich hat -- so I decided to try to make the experience as unpleasant for them as I could.

Poop on Fascists was published earlier this month. It was designed as a joke, and even if you wouldn't dare to wear it in public, I hope it gives you a laugh.

With just over a month until the Norwegian parliamentary elections, I’ve released a free cowl pattern: The Jealous Socialist. Its name was inspired by insults aimed at my "Eat the Rich" hat pattern on Ravelry. Available in both English and Norwegian, The Jealous Socialist's motifs draw on symbols and ideas of the labour movement.
