News for Spring 2024

I have two new patterns out now. Maridal was created in collaboration with one of my favourite yarn companies, Blacker Yarns. Knitted in Swan 4-ply Falkland merino from Blacker, it's a lovely little bit of luxury.

Meanwhile, Ingveld is a riff on the traditional Shetland hap shawl. The garter triangle is knitted sideways on the bias and then stitches are picked up along the side for the lace. The edging features true lace patterned on every row.

I've also written an article -- in Norwegian -- about how participation on foreldrearbeidsutvalg can help immigrants integrate into Norwegian society. It's based on my own experience and observations from my 3 years as a klasserepresentant at my son's school in Oslo.
