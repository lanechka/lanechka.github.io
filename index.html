News for Winter 2024

I have two new patterns out: Vindfell and the 3D Beanie. Vindfell is a triangular shawl with an open garter stitch body and lacy border. It's a pretty easy knit and looks good in laceweight and fingering. Meanwhile, the 3D beanie features stranded colourwork with skulls, leaves, and DELAY - DENY - DEPOSE around the edge.

I've also written an article about redheaded villains in the Hardy Boys mystery novels. There are a lot of them, and although one might wonder if series creator Edward Stratemeyer simply harboured an intense hatred for gingers, there are in fact deep-seated cultural reasons for depicting redhaired malefactors.
