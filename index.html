News for Winter 2024

Inspired by a newly discovered Faroese sweater from the Napoleonic wars, I've designed the Torshavn 1807 mittens. The sweater was originally part of a shipment heading from the Faroe Islands to Denmark in 1807 just as the Napoleonic wars began. The ship was captured by the British navy and taken to the UK as war booty. The sender of the sweater, Niels C. Winther, included a letter that reads in part, “My wife sends her regards, thank you for the pudding rice. She sends your fiance this sweater and hopes that it is not displeasing to her.” Likewise, I hope that these mittens won't be displeasing to you.
