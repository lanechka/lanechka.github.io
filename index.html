News for August 2025

Poop on Fascists is my latest protest pattern as we gear up for fall and winter. It was designed as a joke, and even if you wouldn't dare to wear it in public (I doubt that I would), I hope it gives you a laugh.

With just over a month until the Norwegian parliamentary elections, I’ve released a free cowl pattern: The Jealous Socialist. Its name was inspired by insults aimed at my "Eat the Rich" hat pattern on Ravelry. Available in both English and Norwegian, The Jealous Socialist's motifs draw on symbols and ideas of the labour movement.

In case you missed it, the first pattern in my collaboration with Biscotte Yarns was released last month. The Vøyenfallen shawl is a riff on traditional Shetland half-haps. The garter stitch body is worked sideways on the bias. Then stitches are picked up around the sides for the lace border -- a variation on the traditional Shetland Old Shale pattern. Finally, it’s finished with a simple crochet loop edging.
