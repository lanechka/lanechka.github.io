News for March 2022

The Sunflower Seeds/Solsikkefrø wristers pattern is out now on Ravelry. Knitted in blue and yellow on a white background, it features a Selbu star standing in for a sunflower in support of Ukraine. Available in both English and Norwegian.

The Pattern of the Month for March is Bergtatt. It was inspired by the Norwegian folksong “Margit Hjuxe” in which a troll king kidnaps a young girl in order to marry her. The pattern is 20% off until 31 March 2022; the discount is automatically applied at checkout and you do not need a coupon code.
