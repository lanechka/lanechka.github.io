News for July 2025

Great news for the summer: the first pattern in my 3-pattern collaboration with Biscotte Yarns has just been released! The Vøyenfallen shawl is a riff on traditional Shetland half-haps. The garter stitch body is worked sideways on the bias. Then stitches are picked up around the sides for the lace border -- a variation on the traditional Shetland Old Shale pattern. Finally, it’s finished with a simple crochet loop edging.
