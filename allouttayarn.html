All Outta Yarn: Knitting Collection

This series takes its name from a sign at the 2017 Women's March: "I came here to knit hats + punch Nazis + I'm all outta yarn".

These patterns are not all overtly political but -- with the exception of the 3D hat -- they were designed as a response to Donald Trump's second term as US president. As a dual citizen of Canada and the US who lives in Norway (historically a part of Denmark and for a brief moment in the 1930s a claimant to Greenland), this is the least that I can do.
