Along the Whale Road: Knitting Collection

The “whale road” is a Norse kenning for the sea. This collection highlights yarns from Norway, Denmark, Gotland, Öland, the Isle of Man, Iceland, Shetland, the Orkneys, the Faroes, and the Hebrides -- all places where Norsemen visited, settled, and sometimes conquered.
